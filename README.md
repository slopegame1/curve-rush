<h2>What Is Curve Rush?</h2>

<p>Curve Rush is an endless runner game where you control a ball gliding over curved, undulating slopes. Your goal is to move as far as possible without crashing, all while collecting coins and gaining air points. The longer you last, the higher your score—and the more customization options you can unlock.</p>

<h2>How to Play Curve Rush</h2>

<p>Controlling the ball is simple but demands sharp reflexes:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Hold</strong> the spacebar, up arrow key, or mouse to speed up.</li>
  <li><strong>Release</strong> to fly into the air using the momentum of the slope.</li>
  <li><strong>Tap again</strong> to dive and land smoothly on the next hill.</li>
</ul>
<p>Perfect timing helps you maintain speed and avoid landing on uphill curves, which can slow you down or end your run entirely.</p>

<h2>Achievement Mode Levels</h2>

<p>Curve Rush offers four exciting game styles in Achievement Mode, each with 35 levels to complete:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Normal Mode:</strong> Classic gameplay with right-scrolling terrain.</li>
  <li><strong>Reverse Mode:</strong> Left-scrolling challenges that test your orientation.</li>
  <li><strong>Flip Mode:</strong> A vertically flipped screen for a gravity-defying twist.</li>
  <li><strong>Flip Reverse Mode:</strong> Combines both flipping methods for the ultimate test.</li>
</ul>
<p>Each level offers specific missions and rewards coins for completion, encouraging players to keep progressing.</p>

<h2>Tips to Master Curve Rush</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Time Your Jumps:</strong> Wait until the last moment to jump for longer airtime.</li>
  <li><strong>Land on Downhills:</strong> Avoid slowing down by landing on descending slopes.</li>
  <li><strong>Watch for Air Points:</strong> Fly over white lines to earn extra score boosts.</li>
  <li><strong>Collect Coins:</strong> Grab coins on slopes and in the air to unlock new content.</li>
  <li><strong>Practice Regularly:</strong> The more you play, the better your reactions and timing will become.</li>
</ul>
